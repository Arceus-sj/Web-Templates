STORY OF THE ELON MUSK

Elon Reeve Musk

Born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa.

1981 - 1983

At the age of 10, he devloped an interest in computer with commodore VCL-20 and taught him self computer programming. By the age of 12, Musk sold the code of a Basic-based video game he created called Blaster.

1995 - 1999

After getting a phD in applied physic from Stanford University, he founded his first business, a web software with his brother called Zip2. PC-maker computer offered to pay $307 million in cash for Zip2, from which musk get around $22 million.

2002

He founded Space Exploration Technologies Coorporation i.e.SpaceX in May 2002 and serves as its CEO. Cheif Rocket Desginer and CTO.

2003

Musk founded Tesla Moters in 2003. A company aimed to produce cost-effective electric cars.

2006

SpaceX launches their first rocket. It fails horribly and exploaded while taking off.

2008

Divorce, another failed launch. SpaceX and Tesla both running out of cash. But all the hark work pays off, from September everything change. 4th launch succeeds, and Wins $1.6 B NASA contract.

2015

In late 2015, Musk co-founded OpenAI. A non-profit dedicated to researching artificial intelligence and ensuring it doesn't destroy humanity.

2016

Musk Co-founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology startup company to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence.

2017

Musk unveilied starship and super heavy rocket, which is planned to fully reusable and will be the largest rocket ever on its debut.

2020

The world's most valuable automaker, Tesla was worth nearly $645 Billion. Elon Musk has become the world's richest person, as his net worth crossed $185 Billion.
